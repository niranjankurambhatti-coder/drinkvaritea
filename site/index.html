FAQ

Quick answers, then go drink some tea.

What is the Varitea First Sip Box?

A curated 3-tea starter sampler designed to help you find your daily drink fast. You get one bright green, one bold black, and one calming herbal — all loose-leaf, small-batch, and beginner-friendly. Brewing guide and tasting notes included.

How much tea is in the box?

Three pouches, each brews about 8–10 cups. Plenty to actually live with a tea for a few mornings and figure out what you love.

I drink coffee. Will this actually keep me going?

Yes. The box includes a bold black tea with caffeine in the same neighborhood as a light coffee, plus a green for a smoother lift. Many of our coffee-switching customers use the black tea as a one-to-one morning replacement.

Do you ship for free?

Yes — the $19 First Sip Box ships free in the United States during the founder launch.

Can I give this as a gift?

Yes. The Gift Edition adds a hand-written note card. The box itself is built to feel premium — a clean tea gift box that works for tea drinkers, coffee switchers, and the curious.

Can I drink these teas iced?

Yes. The green and the herbal both make an excellent iced tea starter. Cold-brew instructions are printed on every pouch.
